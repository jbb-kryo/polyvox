## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

**Encryption:**
- Private keys encrypted with AES-256-GCM
- HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
- Encrypted database connections
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)

**Access Controls:**
- Row-level security on all database tables
- Multi-factor authentication support
- Session management
- API authentication

**Monitoring:**
- Security event logging
- Intrusion detection
- Error tracking (sanitized)
- Regular security audits

### 4.2 Local Storage

**Private Keys:**
- Stored encrypted in browser local storage
- Never transmitted to our servers
- Only decrypted when needed for trading
- Protected by master password

**Session Data:**
- Stored in secure browser storage
- Cleared on logout
- Expires after inactivity

### 4.3 Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:
- Keeping your password secure
- Protecting your private keys
- Safeguarding your master password
- Using secure devices and networks
- Logging out when finished
- Reporting security incidents

### 4.4 No Absolute Security

**Important:** No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to:
- Provide continued service
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

### 5.2 Retention Periods

**Account Data:**
- Retained while account is active
- Retained for 90 days after account deletion
- May be retained longer for legal compliance

**Trading History:**
- Retained indefinitely for record-keeping
- May be anonymized after account deletion
- Required for tax and regulatory purposes

**Logs and Analytics:**
- Error logs: 30 days
- Usage analytics: 12 months (anonymized)
- Security logs: 12 months
- Audit trails: 7 years (where required)

### 5.3 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time. Upon request:
- We will delete or anonymize your personal information
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance
- Blockchain data cannot be deleted (permanently public)
- Backups may be retained according to our backup schedule
